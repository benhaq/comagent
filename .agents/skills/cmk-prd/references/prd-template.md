# PRD Template (Portable Baseline)

Use these sections in order unless the target repository has a stronger local convention.

## 1) Problem

- Who has the problem, what it costs them, how they cope today.

## 2) Why Now

- What changed (market, technical, business) that makes this the right time.

## 3) Success Criteria

- Measurable outcomes table: Metric | Target | Measurement Method.

## 4) User Needs and Scenarios

- Core user needs with 1-2 concrete scenarios each.
- Scenarios ground abstract needs in real usage.

## 5) Scope

- In Scope: what this PRD covers.
- Out of Scope: what is excluded with rationale for each exclusion.

## 6) Risks and Assumptions (Optional)

- Product-level risk table: Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Why It Exists | Mitigation.
- Assumptions with "what breaks if wrong."

## 7) Constraints (Optional)

- Business, regulatory, or timeline givens.

## 8) Open Points (Optional)

- Unresolved product decisions with context and options.

## 9) Related Documents (Optional)

- Links to system-design, codebase-summary.
- Downstream Specs subsection tracking feature specs that implement this PRD.
